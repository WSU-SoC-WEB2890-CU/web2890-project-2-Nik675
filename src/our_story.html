Our Story

Javier’s story is the American Dream. Javier Chavez was born and raised in a poor village near Zacatecas, Mexico. Javier grew up on a small ranch and at a very young age learned the values of hard work and overcoming challenges, values he would use to build Javier’s Authentic Mexican Food restaurants. Javier’s secret to success has always been to provide his guests with excellent Mexican food and friendly service. Javier wants to make sure everyone feels at home, he treats everyone like family. Javier has put his heart and soul into creating a family environment where everybody feels welcome and everybody can enjoy great enchiladas and tacos.
